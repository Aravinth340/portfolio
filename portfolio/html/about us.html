About Us

WELCOME TO CHENDHURAN POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

Chendhuran Polytechnic College is a Self Financing Polytechnic, governed by Tiruchendhuran Murugan Trust. The polytechnic college has been commenced in the year 2007, with an intention of granting opportunity for all the students to bolster their educational aspirations and seek to flourish their interpersonal skills. The college is located in a serene 23-acre campus, lush and green in the Pudukkottai Madurai highway at Lena Vilaku, a suburb of Pudukkottai District.
