PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

WELCOME TO CHENDHURAN POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

First of all, I thank you for visiting our Polytechnic College's website.

According to our Former President Dr ABJ Abdul KalamÂ "Education is an endless journey through knowledge and enlightenment. Education system should retain the smile on the faces our children".

To bring the above words in to reality, our college was established by Tiruchendhur Murugan Trust and It is functioning effectively under the guidance of the Management and Governing Council which comprises of eminent personalities as its members.

Technical education is the foundation for the prosperity and self-sufficiency of every country. As rightly observed by many Technocrats, the technical education must have latest curriculum with true assessment strategies built with latest infrastructure facilities. These factors decide the quality of the candidates after completing their technical education. In order to meet out the everchanging expectations of society and industry, various measures are being taken at our institute.

Innovative teaching methodologies (Outcome Based Education) are being adopted to bring out the inherent potentials of our students. Among all the Electives included in the DOTE syllabus, the electives that are highly required for the industry are taught as per the prerequisite of industries by signing MoU with leading industries. Theoretical knowledge of students is enhanced by imparting practical training in state-of-the-art infrastructure facilities provided in laboratories and workshops.

Class rooms are provided with multimedia gadgets and LCD Projectors to deliver improved teaching-learning process. Well-maintained classrooms with good ambiance stimulate the students to learn things in a pleasant manner. A well-stacked library helps the students to attain highest academic standards. The campus has adequate sports infrastructure to enable the students to display their talents in sports arena also. Clubs and Societies such as Department wise Student's Association, Institution of Engineers (India) provide opportunities to students to display their knowledge and skill in curricular and co-curricular activities.

An active placement cell is functioning dedicatedly to provide appropriate placement opportunities to all students. The main focus is to empower the students with sound technical knowledge, Practical skill and adequate training to enable them to face the real world and excel in their study.

I am pleased to assure that this renowned institution is marching towards attaining excellence and attaining its position in the list of top technical institutions at diploma level. With the unstinted support from Management and selfless efforts by our dedicated faculty, the institution is targeted to achieve many crowns in the years to come. It is my assurance that success is inevitable when executing disciplined smart work with dedication and commitment towards achieving proper vision.

Once again, I submit my sincere thanks for visiting our college website
